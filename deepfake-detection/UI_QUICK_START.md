## 🎓 Understanding the Results

### Trust Score
- **90-100%:** Real with high confidence
- **70-89%:** Likely real
- **50-69%:** Uncertain
- **30-49%:** Likely fake
- **0-29%:** Fake with high confidence

### Artifacts Found
- **Facial:** Abnormal feature blending
- **Lighting:** Inconsistent illumination
- **Color:** Unnatural color gradients
- **Motion:** Unnatural movement patterns
- **Audio:** Synthetic speech characteristics

### Confidence Breakdown
- **Model 1:** Individual model confidence
- **Model 2:** Secondary analysis
- **Fusion:** Weighted combination
- **Final:** Overall trust assessment
