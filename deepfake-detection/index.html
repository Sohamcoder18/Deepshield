About DeepShield

DeepShield is a state-of-the-art deepfake detection system designed to help identify manipulated multimedia content. With advances in AI and deep learning, creating convincing deepfakes has become easier, making detection tools essential for content verification and misinformation prevention.

Our system combines multiple detection algorithms and machine learning models to provide comprehensive analysis across images, videos, and audio content. We leverage cutting-edge techniques like XceptionNet, MTCNN, and MFCC feature extraction to ensure accurate and reliable results.

DeepShield is committed to transparency and explainability, providing visual insights into why content was flagged as potentially fake, helping users understand the detection process.
